Our Philosophy

Moving away from treating conditions, towards treating people.

Chronic and persistent pain is one of the most underestimated challenges of modern life. At Solis, we believe the traditional clinical model — cold, transactional, condition-focused — fails people at the most fundamental level. We built something different: a calm, boutique space where you are seen as a whole person, not a set of symptoms to be managed.

What we stand for
01

Education over dependency

We empower clients with an understanding of their own body — so they leave more capable, not more reliant. Knowledge is part of the treatment.

02

Calm, intentional space

Our environment is deliberately unhurried. No crowds, no noise, no performance. Just focused, present attention — to the session and to you.

03

Strength as medicine

We view movement and strength as fundamental to long-term health — physical and mental. Building resilience isn't separate from recovery. It is recovery.

Dom Founder
The person behind Solis

Built from
a conviction.

Solis was founded on a simple but deeply held belief: that the health system too often treats the condition and overlooks the person. Dom set out to create something that didn't exist in Perth — a private, boutique studio where clinical rigour and genuine human care exist in the same space.

Every session at Solis reflects that conviction. No templates, no shortcuts, no rushing through. Just focused, evidence-informed practice in an environment designed to make you feel at ease from the moment you walk in.
