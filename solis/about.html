The Space.

SOLIS is not a commercial gym - and it was never meant to be. Housed in a warehouse in the heart of Subiaco, the studio has been purposefully designed from the ground up to feel unlike anything else in Perth. Hand-picked high-end equipment chosen for quality over quantity, plants throughout, and a calm industrial aesthetic that is as intentional as the work that happens inside it.

Our Philosophy

We've built an environment where pain and mental health are addressed together, free from the clinical language, fear and limitation that conventional healthcare too often creates. Remedial massage and structured training work together here to remove the physical and mental barriers that keep people stuck - replacing confusion and apprehension with structure, strength and clarity. We don't focus on what's wrong or what you can't do. We find what you can recover from, build a baseline, and grow from there - in a calm, intentional environment where you feel empowered, supported and genuinely taken care of.

What we stand for

Resilience.

Whatever you're dealing with - pain, injury, stress, fatigue - the answer is almost always to build more resilience, not do less. The body and mind become more resilient through the same process: controlled exposure, progressive challenge, and recovery. Progressive training develops tolerance at a neurological level, the goal is not to avoid discomfort but to expand what you can tolerate, until what once felt overwhelming becomes manageable. This increased resilience, built in the gym, transcends into all aspects of life, leaving clients empowered and more equipped for whatever the day brings.

02

Intention.

In a world that rewards speed and productivity, there is something quietly powerful about slowing down and doing one thing well.

In a world that rewards speed and productivity, there is something quietly powerful about slowing down and doing one thing well. At SOLIS, every session is built around intentional movement and that intention is where physical and mental progress meet. Training with presence, fully focused on the task in front of you, is mindfulness in practice. It is also where the best results come from - because when the mind and body are working together toward the same goal, progress accelerates. Acting with intention and clarity is woven into everything we do here at SOLIS.

Movement is the most powerful intervention available to the human body and mind and the evidence is clear. Nothing produces better outcomes for chronic pain, mental health and long term quality of life than purposeful, progressive movement applied consistently over time. Not medication, not passive treatment, not rest. Movement. At SOLIS, movement isn’t something you earn once you’re feeling better. It’s the reason you feel better. Every session is designed to meet you where you are, removing the barriers that have been keeping you stuck and building from there with structure, intention and care. Through this method, things that seemed out of reach - strength, a physique you’re proud of, freedom of movement, a quieter mind - begin to feel not only achievable but inevitable.

Built from

Solis was founded on a simple but deeply held belief: that the health system too often treats the condition and overlooks the person. Dom set out to create something that didn't exist in Perth — a private, boutique studio where clinical rigour and genuine human care exist in the same space.

Every session at Solis reflects that conviction. No templates, no shortcuts, no rushing through. Just focused, evidence-informed practice in an environment designed to make you feel at ease from the moment you walk in.
