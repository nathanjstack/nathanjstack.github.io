Cancellation
Policy

We understand that life can be unpredictable. Here's what you need to know if you need to cancel or reschedule.

01 Notice Period
  • 12 Hours Notice: We require a minimum of 12 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling of appointments.
  • Less Than 12 Hours: Cancellations or rescheduling requests made less than 12 hours before the scheduled appointment time will incur a cancellation fee.
02 Cancellation Fees
  • Less Than 12-Hour Cancellations: If you cancel on the day of your appointment you will be charged 50% of the scheduled service fee.
  • No-Shows: If you do not show up for your appointment without prior notice, you will be charged 100% of the scheduled service fee.
  • Late Arrivals: If you arrive late without prior notice, we may not be able to accommodate the full session. The full service fee will still apply.
03 How to Cancel or Reschedule
  • Phone: Call or text us on 0434 415 080 to cancel or reschedule your appointment.
  • Email: Send an email to info@solisperth.com.au with your name, appointment date, time, and your request to cancel or reschedule.
04 Exceptions

We understand that emergencies and unforeseen circumstances can occur. Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss any extenuating circumstances. We may waive the cancellation fee at our discretion in such cases.

05 Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation of your cancellation or rescheduling via email or phone. If you do not receive confirmation, please follow up to ensure your request has been processed.

06 Multiple Cancellations

Repeated cancellations or no-shows may result in a non-refundable deposit being required for future bookings.
