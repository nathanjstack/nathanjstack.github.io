FAQs

Answers to common questions about Solis Health & Wellness.

How long does a session last? ⌄

Your first session and treatment lasts one hour. Follow-up visits are either 45 or 60 minutes depending on your preference and your therapist's recommendation.

How many sessions do I need to do? ⌄

After your initial assessment, your Solis therapist will provide you with a recommendation based on your needs. The majority of our clients come in to either fix their pain, recover from injuries, or tune-up their bodies as part of their monthly routine. If you are in pain, our therapists will be able to provide a more specific recommendation catered to your needs.

What do I need to bring for my session? ⌄

Please bring comfortable athletic clothing for your treatment session. We ask that you bring a towel for your strength and conditioning session.

Is Solis covered by my insurance? ⌄

Our services are provided by licensed remedial therapists and may be covered by your private health provider. Our front desk team can work with you to help you take advantage of any benefits your insurance company may provide.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my session? ⌄

Per our cancellation policy, if you need to reschedule or cancel your session we require 12 hour advance notice. If you cancel or reschedule your session within 12 hours, you will be charged for the session. Our full cancellation policy can be found here.

Is there onsite parking? ⌄

Yes, there is street parking on Axon St.
