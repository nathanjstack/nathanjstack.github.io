Remedial Massage ⌄

We have over (x) years of experience in addressing the pain points of our clients through remedial massage. Each session begins with a thorough assessment of your issues and goals, followed by manual therapy, utilising a range of techniques based on your situation, including cupping, joint mobilisations, deep tissue massage and dry needling, all of which are informed and backed by the latest scientific research. You will then be provided with a personalised treatment plan on how your pain can be managed moving forwards.

This process of identifying and remedying your pain will have a cascading effect on almost every facet of your life, supplementing your general and mental health, resilience, performance, and more.

Personal Training ⌄

At SOLIS, we offer 1:1 or 2:1 personal training sessions. For your first session, you will be taken through a range of preliminary baseline tests to understand what works well for you, what doesn't, and what your goals are, whether they are anything from aesthetic desires to improving your general health, they will always be our main priority. We then consider all of this information to incrementally create a targeted and deeply personalised program for you across each session, adjusted intentionally to your specific anatomy, preferences, and movement patterns that work well for you. This will help you to achieve a rate of progress that would never be possible with a generic pre-made program.

Recovery ⌄

Resistance training sets you up for growth, while proper recovery ensures it. At SOLIS, we accommodate for your recovery needs with our private infrared sauna, which uses light to heat your body directly, delivering a range of health benefits such as accelerated muscle recovery, improved circulation, relaxation, and improving heart health. These are bookable exclusively for members, with a maximum of 2 people per session.
