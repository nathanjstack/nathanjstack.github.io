Services

Comprehensive wellness solutions designed to support your health and fitness goals. From remedial massage to personal training and recovery, we've got you covered.

Remedial Massage ⌄

Utilising a deep tissue targeting technique, remedial massages treat chronic pain, stiffness and muscle damage improving mobility and assisting with injury rehabilitation. At SOLIS, each session begins with the establishment and assessment of your current pain points and intended outcomes, followed by manual therapy, utilising a range of techniques tailored to each individuals needs and restrictions. We also offer cupping, joint mobilisations and dry needling, all of which are informed and backed by the latest scientific research. The session concludes with a personalised treatment plan to address pain managment moving forwards.

Personal Training ⌄

At SOLIS, we view personal training as a way for people to build strength and resilience physically and mentally. Offering 1:1 and 1:2 personalised training sessions, each new client is taken through a range of baseline tests to aid and understand potential limitations and opportunities for growth. We encourage the development of individual goals, which may be anything from aesthetic desires to managing chronic pain, and through this knowledge establish the perfect personalised program created intentionally to target these specific desires. Possessing a wide range of high quality equipment, including LifeFitness, Nautilus and Hammer Strength machines, and the expert knowledge of the trainers, we achieve a rate of progress that would never be possible with a generic pre-made program.

Prioritising efficacy and mindful movement, we strive to offer a space and time for clients to draw focus to the present moment and on the movements they are doing, offering a moment of peace away from everyday demands.

Recovery ⌄

While Resistance training sets you up for growth, proper recovery ensures it. At SOLIS, we accommodate for your recovery needs with our private infrared sauna, which uses light to heat your body directly, delivering a range of health benefits such as accelerated muscle recovery, improved circulation, relaxation, and improving heart health. These can be booked prior, with a maximum of 2 people per session.
